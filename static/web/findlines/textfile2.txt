Route 308 is a 6.19-mile-long (9.96 km) state 
highway within northern Dutchess County in 
New York State that connects U.S. Route 9 and
NY 9G. The western end of the highway is in 
the Rhinebeck Village Historic District, 
a 2.6-square-mile (6.7 km2) area that features 
272 buildings in a variety of architectural styles.
NY 308 passes near the Dutchess County Fairgrounds, 
several historical landmarks, and the Landsman Kill 
River,a minor tributary of the Hudson River.
